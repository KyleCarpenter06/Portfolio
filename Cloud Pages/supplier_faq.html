Frequently Asked Questions from Suppliers

Question Answer
I submitted an invoice but have not been paid. What should I do?

Please contact our Supplier Help Desk through the CompanyName Accounts Payable portal.

After you have registered (if first time visiting the portal) and logged in, go to the “How to Get Help?” section to submit a case in writing.

Alternatively, you can check the “Call our Customer Service Help Desk” section to find the appropriate Help Desk phone number for your country.

Our Supplier Help Desk representative will provide the status of your payment; or, if necessary, assign your case to the appropriate CompanyName business team.
What will happen if I don’t submit my invoice by June 24?

You will have to submit your invoice against the new PO once it has been issued (starting July 4).
What will change when the new POs are issued?

The Legal Entity (LE) name, Bill To address and PO numbers will change.
What do I do if I have invoice mismatches?

Please contact our Supplier Help Desk through the CompanyName Accounts Payable portal.

After you have registered (if first time visiting the portal) and logged in, go to the “How to Get Help?” section to submit a case in writing.

Alternatively, you can check the “Call our Customer Service Help Desk” section to find the appropriate Help Desk phone number for your country.

Our Supplier Help Desk representative will provide the status of your payment; or, if necessary, assign your case to the appropriate CompanyName business team.
How do I obtain a new PO?

We will provide you with the new Purchase Order. Please follow up with your business contact if you do not receive it.
How do I add on to a closed Purchase Order (PO)?

You will not be able to invoice against a closed purchase order (PO). You will receive a new PO, against which you can invoice.

Contact our Supplier Help Desk if you do not receive a new PO.
How do I submit an invoice to the new PO?

Please use your current invoicing channel to submit your new PO.

If you are not using our electronic invoicing system, be aware that the new Legal Entities will have a new postal address.

CompanyName encourages the use of web invoicing wherever possible and permissible. See the CompanyName Accounts Payable portal for more information.
Will my payment terms change with the change to a new Legal Entity?

No, your payment terms will not change with the move to a new Legal Entity. Your current payment terms will remain the same as agreed in the contract or mentioned in the Purchase Order.
