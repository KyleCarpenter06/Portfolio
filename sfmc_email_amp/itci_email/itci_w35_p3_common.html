Support throughout the migration process will include:
• Transferring your OneDrive, email files and history to your new J&J account.
• Replacing your Intra-Cellular PC and/or iPad with a J&J device.
• Setting up your current mobile devices to access applicable J&J systems.
• Accessing Intra-Cellular MS Teams and SharePoint sites.
• Rescheduling previously created Teams and Zoom meetings using your new J&J account.
• Providing access to apps via the J&J app store.
