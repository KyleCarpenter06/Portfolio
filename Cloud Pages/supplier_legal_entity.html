New Legal Entities Created for New Consumer Health Company in Finland and Norway
Last November we announced our intent to separate CompanyName Services, Inc.'s Consumer Health business, creating a new publicly traded company. Our Pharmaceutical and MedTech businesses would continue to operate as CompanyName Services, Inc. This should help us achieve accelerated growth for both companies, and we look forward to sharing that growth with you.
To support the planned separation, certain new Legal Entities (LEs) have been created for the new Consumer Health Company in Finland and Norway.
What you need to know
  • All valid POs will be closed and replaced with newly created POs, which will include a change in the PO number.
  • During the period September 19 – October 2, POs will not be issued
  • Beginning October 3, we will separate CompanyName Services, Inc.'s Consumer Health business into new Legal Entities, and POs will be issued automatically under the new Legal Entities, with new PO numbers. We will communicate this information to you in a separate email.
What you need to do
  • Please submit final invoices as soon as possible and no later than September 22. Invoices submitted after September 22 must be issued against the newly created POs and billed to the new Legal Entity beginning October 3.
  • Set up the new Legal Entity in your customer records according to the country you are doing business with.
  • Beginning October 3, we will separate CompanyName Services, Inc.'s Consumer Health business into new Legal Entities, and POs will be issued automatically under the new Legal Entities, with new PO numbers. We will communicate this information to you in a separate email.
